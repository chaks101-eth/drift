# Product Hunt Launch — Drift

## Tagline
AI travel planner that watches your reels and builds real trips

## Description
Drift turns travel inspiration into action. Paste a YouTube or Instagram travel reel — our AI watches it frame by frame, identifies every place, and builds a complete trip with real venue photos, Google ratings, weather-aware scheduling, and booking links.

**How it works:**
1. Tell us your vibes (beach, culture, foodie, adventure)
2. Pick a destination — or paste a travel reel
3. AI builds a day-by-day itinerary with real data from Google Places
4. Every item has real photos, ratings, GPS coordinates, and booking links
5. Chat with AI to swap items, adjust budget, or ask about your trip

**What makes Drift different from ChatGPT:**
- Real venue photos from Google Places (not stock images)
- Weather-aware scheduling (outdoor activities on sunny days)
- Visual day maps with numbered pins
- AI chat with 8 tools that actually modify your trip
- Paste any travel reel → bookable trip in 30 seconds
- Quality-scored itineraries (avg 96/100 across 20 destinations)

**Built with:**
Gemini 2.5 Flash, Google Places API, Google Weather API, Maps Static API, Google Routes API, Amadeus flights, Supabase, Next.js 16
