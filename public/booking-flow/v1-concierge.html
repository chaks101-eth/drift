PRO TIP
The email link opens a Drift-branded payment page. Stripe handles the card. Confirmation comes back from Drift, not the airline.

The payment lives outside the app on the first MVP — a Stripe Checkout link in the email. Drift wraps everything in its brand.

Wins

Ships in 2 weeks. Zero API contracts, zero PCI scope, zero Amadeus/Duffel integration. The "5 min concierge" framing is a feature, not a bug — it builds trust.

Costs

Manual labor doesn't scale past ~100 bookings/day. Out-of-band payment loses the "in-app feel" for the actual money moment. Affiliate revenue only — no margin on the inventory.

Dependencies

Stripe Checkout, Resend (already wired), and a manual queue (Airtable or Linear). Optional: Skyscanner/Travelpayouts affiliate link for tracked revenue.
